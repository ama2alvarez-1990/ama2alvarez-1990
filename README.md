### What is ARIS4U

ARIS4U is a local AI co-processor I built to augment how I work. It runs entirely on a MacBook Pro with no cloud dependency for reasoning.

At its core is a knowledge brain with over 13,000 curated, high-quality knowledge atoms. Think of atoms as the building blocks of understanding: each one is a verified fact extracted from research papers, technical documentation, or real-world experience, with a confidence score, a source, and typed connections to related atoms across different fields. To put it in perspective, it's the equivalent of having distilled insights from hundreds of research papers and technical books into a structured, searchable, cross-referenced knowledge graph that the system can reason over instantly.

These atoms span 66 domains (from AI/ML and security to healthcare compliance and financial modeling), and ARIS4U uses them to generate code in 6 frameworks in under 5ms, scan for PHI/PII in under 1ms, and orchestrate tasks across machines via SSH.

It's not a product. It's the system I use every day to ship real projects faster than should be possible for one person.
